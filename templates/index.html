INSTRUCTIONS

  • Airport includes of foreign key from city table known as city_name.
  • Airport_consist_of includes a foreign key from airport and airline table.
  • Air_ID is the foreign key to refer in Airline in Flight table.
  • In Flight_info, FNo is the foreign key to refer in Flight and NB_ID is the foreign key to refer in Notice_board.
  • In Flight_Passenger_transmits, FNo, P_ID and NB_ID are the foreign key to refer Flight, Passenger and Notice_board respectively.
  • In Employee, A_Name are the foreign key.
  • In Employee_handles, Emp_ID and P_ID are the foreign key refer to Employee and Passenger respectively.
  • In Ticket, P_ID is the foreign key to refer in Passenger.
  • In Admin_Support, Emp_ID is the foreign key taken from employee table.
  • In Engineer, Emp_ID is the foreign key taken from employee table.
  • In Luggage, P_ID is the foreign key taken from passenger table.
  • In Connecting_Flight and Non_Stop_Flight, FNo is the foreign key taken from flight table.
